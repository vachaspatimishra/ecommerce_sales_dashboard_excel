# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Intelligence Dashboard

# 🎯 Objective
To analyze e-commerce sales data and generate actionable business insights using Excel.

# 🧩 Business Problem
An e-commerce company wants to analyze its sales performance to identify key revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, and operational inefficiencies.

The objective is to:
* Identify top-performing product categories and regions
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior and payment preferences
* Detect operational issues such as high order cancellations
* Provide actionable insights to improve revenue and efficiency

# 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
* Order details (Order ID, Date, Quantity, Total Amount)
* Customer information
* Product categories and pricing
* Region-wise sales data
* Payment methods
* Delivery status and performance

# 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used
* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers
* KPI Metrics
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

# 📊 Dashboard Features
* 📈 Revenue & Order KPIs
* 🌍 Region-wise Sales Analysis
* 🛍 Category Performance Breakdown
* 💳 Payment Method Distribution
* 🚚 Delivery Status Tracking
* 📅 Monthly Sales Trends

# 🔍 Key Insights
* Generated total revenue of ₹35K across 100 orders with an average order value of ₹352
* Electronics category contributed ~43% of total revenue, indicating high dependency
* North region emerged as the top-performing region, while East showed lowest sales
* Identified a high cancellation rate (~30%), suggesting operational inefficiencies
* Digital payment methods dominated transactions, with Apple Pay being the most used
* Slow delivery performance may be contributing to higher cancellation rates

# 📈 Business Recommendations
* Reduce dependency on Electronics by promoting other categories like Fashion
* Improve logistics to reduce delivery delays and cancellations
* Focus marketing efforts on underperforming regions (East & Central)
* Optimize fulfillment partner performance
* Introduce customer retention strategies for repeat purchases
